About AIAP

The Association of Inventory Academicians and Practitioners (AIAP) aims to promote research and awareness of Inventory Control and Management in Industry, Society, and Educational and Research Development Organisations. It provides a common platform for Indian academicians and practitioners involved in Inventory Control and Management. The organization conducts conferences, seminars, workshops, and other events to foster knowledge exchange and address front-line research areas. AIAP publishes books, journals, manuals, and periodicals to develop a quantitative culture and promote inventory control techniques. It organizes gatherings of experts and stakeholders to spread the importance of inventory control. The establishment of the School of Quantitative Science encourages research and employment opportunities. Learn More
