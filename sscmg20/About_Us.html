ABOUT

The Association of Inventory Academicians and Practitioners (AIAP) is pleased to present the International Conference on Sustainable Supply Chain Management under India's G20 Presidency (SSCM-G20), in collaboration with the Department of Operational Research at the prestigious University of Delhi.

Association of Inventory Academicians and Practitioners (AIAP)
 xyz
 With a vision of providing a forum for researchers, academicians, industry professionals and scientists working in the area of Inventory Management and related disciplines to widen their horizon by exchange of knowledge and application of techniques, Prof. Chandra K. Jaggi founded the Association of Inventory Academicians and Practitioners (AIAP) in 2014 with its office at New Delhi, India. The society aims to generate awareness and disseminate knowledge for improving industrial practice and to strengthen the theoretical base necessary for support in the field of Inventory Control and Management.
(For more information visit AIAP)

Department of Operational Research, University of Delhi
xyz
 The University of Delhi is the premier university of India and is known for its high standards in teaching and research and attracts eminent scholars to its faculty. It was established in 1922 with extensive research libraries and cultural and recreational facilities, the university attracts students from all corners of the globe.

The Department of Operational Research was established in 1973. Department is a pioneer department to start Post Graduate course in Operational Research in India. The department is renowned for its high standards in teaching, research, and scholarly achievement. The faculty consists of internationally recognized researchers with considerable expertise in applying OR and statistical methods.
(For more information visit Delhi University)
