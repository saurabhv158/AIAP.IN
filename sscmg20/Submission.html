Call for Papers

All the authors are encouraged to contribute their work through abstract and papers that address the theme of the conference. High quality research contributions describing original and unpublished work in any one/combined areas of the theme of the conference are welcomed.

Guidelines for abstract

Abstract should be informative explaining the background, the need, aims, methods and the expected findings. The last date for submission of abstract is October 27, 2023.
The abstract should be submitted as word file (.docx) through email at SSCMG20@gmail.com
  • Abstract should contain Time New Roman Theme Fond with Font size 12.
  • Abstract should be written with the following elements in the following order: Title; Author(s) Name; Affiliation; abstract; keywords; Email
  • Abstract should contain an unstructured abstract of 200 words.
  • Abstract should contain between 5 and 6 keywords.

Best Paper Awards

The "Best Paper Awards" will be given to the research papers which will be selected by the committee among the submitted (latest by November 02, 2023) and presented papers in SSCM-G20. The nominees will be evaluated with blind peer review system. For the evaluation process scientific contribution, managerial implication, writing/presentation style will be considered. Top Three papers will be selected for the Best Paper Award category.
