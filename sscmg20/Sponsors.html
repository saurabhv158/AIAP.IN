SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITY

This is a rare opportunity for all to spend a quality time in listening to and interacting with many of the best brains in the world on an extraordinary range of topics and issues. The subject of the conference will encourage a domain for pondering on various research aspects on the said theme. In this all-around focused condition, sustainable supply chain management are the key issues in accomplishing market administration. Stock Management and Supply Chain is indivisible piece of any production or distribution system. Stock Management helps in deciding ideal stock levels while Supply Chain decides the ideal stream of data and material from place of production to place of necessity. Reliability issues are likewise of prime significance in the development of high quality software, design of systems and processes.
