International Conference on Sustainable Supply Chain Management under India's G20 Presidency (SSCM-G20)

  • Date: November 4-5, 2023
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Place: Conference Centre, University of Delhi, Delhi, India-110007

The conference's objective is to emphasize the role of Operational Research for the G20 key goals on sustainable supply chain development: to apply mathematical and analytical techniques to optimize and enhance supply chain operations to combat climate change. SSCM-G20 aims to foster global collaboration and innovative solutions in creating resilient and environmentally responsible supply chains under India's G20 agenda. The conference intends to bring together industry experts, academicians, professionals, and scientists to discuss and address challenges concerning responsible sourcing, green logistics, circular economy practices, and social inclusivity in supply chains which are the key objectives of G20. Participants will have the chance to delve into discussions and keynote sessions that offer insights into nurturing a sustainable environment to enhance supply chain operations.

  • Place: Satyakam Bhawan,Social Sciences Extension Building University of Delhi, Delhi, India-110007

Workshop on HOW TO WRITE A RESEARCH PAPER

  • Date: December 17-20, 2018
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Place: Department of Operational Research University of Delhi, Delhi, India-110007

It is well known that the main objective of the scientific research is the publication of the results in papers, books, technical reports, thesis, among others sources of dissemination. Every researcher requires to do a good scientific writing. However, writing a paper is a challenge task that requires a lot of creative work.
